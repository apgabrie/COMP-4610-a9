Scrabble

Tiles can only be placed on valid spaces, which are highlighted in green. The Return Tiles button returns all tiles that are currently on the board to the rack. The New Hand button exchanges all tiles currently on the rack with a random one from the remaining tiles. The End Turn Button ends the turn and attempts to validate all words created. The New Game button resets everything, removing all tiles from the board, and setting the score to 0.

  • ADDITIONAL NOTES:
  • As per the rules of Scrabble, the starting space counts as a "Double Word Score" space.
  • Unlike in Scrabble, Blank tiles count as any letter, even for different words.
  • Graphics do not display correctly after zooming in or out with your browser.
  • This application was only tested in Firefox.
